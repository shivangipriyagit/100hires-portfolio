# B2B SaaS Marketing Research Project
# Overview:

This repository contains a research project focused on understanding effective marketing strategies for B2B SaaS companies.
The goal of this project was to study how experienced practitioners approach growth, content, acquisition, and customer engagement by analyzing their real-world content, including LinkedIn posts, YouTube videos, and other marketing resources.

## Selected Topic
AI-Powered SEO Content Production

## Research Objective
The objective of this research was to understand how AI is transforming SEO content workflows, including:

- AI-assisted keyword research and content planning
- Content creation workflows using AI tools
- Human + AI collaboration for high-quality SEO content
- Scaling content production while maintaining quality
- SEO strategies used by successful B2B SaaS companies

## Expert Selection

I researched 10 practitioners and creators who actively work in the SEO, AI marketing, and B2B SaaS growth space.

The experts were selected based on:

- Practical experience in implementing SEO and content strategies
- Consistent publishing of educational content
- Real-world case studies and frameworks
- Experience working with SaaS businesses and marketing teams

## Research Collection Process

The research data was collected from multiple sources:

- YouTube transcripts collected using API-based extraction methods
- LinkedIn posts collected manually and through available scraping methods
- Additional articles, frameworks, and marketing resources

The collected information was organized into:

    └── [Additional research materials]

# Key Learnings

Through this research, I analyzed:

- How experts structure SEO content systems
- How AI tools are integrated into marketing workflows
- Common patterns behind successful content strategies
- Opportunities for automation in SEO production
- The balance between AI efficiency and human expertise

# Tools & Methods Used

- GitHub for research organization and documentation
- Claude Code / Codex for research assistance and automation
- YouTube transcript APIs for content extraction
- LinkedIn content analysis
- Markdown documentation

# Repository Structure

This repository is continuously updated as new insights and resources are collected.

The purpose of this project is to build a practical understanding of modern AI-driven marketing workflows and apply these learnings to B2B SaaS growth strategies.
